About Blockchain Days

ODTU Blockchain presents Blockchain Days. Blockchain Days aims to gather diffrent communities and experienced people in one event. Through two days we will be listening to succesfull people around web3 community and building new networks. This will be the largest blockchain event and we as ODTU Blockchain are excited, proud to be organizing it. For further announcement keep checking our website.
